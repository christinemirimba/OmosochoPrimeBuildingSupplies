Our Story

Founded in 2009, Omosocho Prime began as a small family business in Nyamache with a simple mission: to provide quality tools and expert advice to our community in Kisii County.

What started as a modest storefront has grown into a comprehensive hardware solution center, serving everyone from weekend warriors to large construction companies across the region.

Today, we're proud to be known for our extensive inventory, competitive prices, and most importantly, our knowledgeable staff who are always ready to help you find exactly what you need.
